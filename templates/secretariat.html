Meet the Secretariat

The Secretariat is a thirteen-person team tasked with running the entire conference. From overseeing the tens of staff members to liaising with the hundreds of delegates that attend the conference, it is their leadership that makes TLMUN the most well-run, prestigious Model UN conference in Malaysia.
